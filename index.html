Privacy Policy for SpamBlocker Last updated: June 03, 2024 This Privacy Policy describes the data collection practices of SpamBlocker app. 1. No Data Collection SpamBlocker does not collect any personal information from users. This includes but is not limited to: Contact information (email address, phone number) Account information (username, password) Usage data (app usage patterns, features used) Device information (device type, operating system, IP address) The App functions entirely offline and does not require any internet connection or user accounts. 2. Commitment to Privacy We are committed to protecting your privacy by design. The App is developed with the intention of providing a valuable experience without the need to collect any user data. 3. Third-Party Services The App does not integrate with any third-party services that might collect user data. 4. Contact Information If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please fire an issue on https://github.com/aj3423/SpamBlocker/issues.
